About Me

Hi, my name is Jackie Zhou and I'm currently a sophomore at the University of Michigan. For my final project, I wanted to showcase the albums and music that I find interesting and the meaning behind them and what each one means to me.

What Music Means to Me

Music to me is a way for my mind to put into a certain mood or feel a certain way. My music taste has changed and evolved as I grew up and experienced different perspectives that change my outlook on what I find interesting to listen to. Currently, I enjoy R&B and Pop, but I'm also into some hyperpop songs. My taste is constantly changing, showing the dynamic nature of music and how different it is for each person.
